About Me

I'm Scarlett Medina, a Computer Science graduate with a passion for creating clean, user-focused web applications. My journey in tech began with a curiosity about how things work — and grew into a drive to solve real-world problems through code.

I enjoy building full-stack applications, especially those involving Java, APIs, and front-end interfaces. I’m particularly drawn to projects that impact people’s lives and simplify complex processes.

Outside of programming, I love traveling, going to the gym, and learning languages — I recently studied abroad in Spain and speak both English and Spanish fluently.
